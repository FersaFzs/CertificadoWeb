THEWEEKND

Top 1 artist on Spotify

Abel Tesfaye, better known as The Weeknd, is the visionary artist who redefined the sound of modern R&B. Born in Toronto, Canada, he began his journey by anonymously uploading haunting, emotional tracks to YouTube in 2010. His mysterious persona and unique blend of dark lyrics, smooth vocals, and atmospheric production quickly caught the attention of music lovers around the world. Over the years, The Weeknd has evolved into a global superstar, constantly pushing the boundaries of his artistry while staying true to his raw, emotional roots.
